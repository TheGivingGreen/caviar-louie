Frequently Asked Questions about Caviar Louie

What is Caviar Louie?

Caviar Louie is St. Louis' only mobile caviar bar. We bring a fully staffed tableside caviar experience directly to your event, venue, or celebration anywhere in St. Louis, Missouri.

How does the caviar cart work?

Our cart arrives fully stocked with premium caviar, mother of pearl spoons, traditional accompaniments, and grower champagne. Our team sets up, serves, and handles everything so you can focus on your guests.

What events does Caviar Louie serve?

We serve birthdays, bachelorette parties, weddings, graduations, mitzvahs, corporate events, happy hours, soirées, pop-ups, and any celebration worth remembering in St. Louis.

Where is Caviar Louie located?

Caviar Louie is based in St. Louis, Missouri and serves the greater St. Louis metropolitan area.

How do I book Caviar Louie for my event?

Fill out our inquiry form at caviarlouie.com with your event date, guest count, and vision. We will customize a caviar experience around your celebration and respond within 24 hours.

How much does Caviar Louie cost?

Pricing varies based on guest count, duration, and customization. Contact us at hello@caviarlouie.com for a custom quote.

Is Caviar Louie available for weddings in St. Louis?

Yes. Caviar Louie provides full caviar service for weddings throughout St. Louis and the surrounding area. We customize every detail to match your wedding vision.

What makes Caviar Louie different?

Caviar Louie is St. Louis' only mobile caviar bar. We come to you. Our cart arrives at your venue fully staffed and ready to deliver a world-class caviar experience without you lifting a finger.
