About

Hi there,

Welcome to Caviar Louie. We're so happy you're here.

We believe caviar is the tin of magic that transforms a beautiful gathering into an unforgettable moment. Elegant, indulgent, and effortlessly celebratory, it has a way of making every occasion feel just a little more iconic.

From rooftop soirées, pop-ups, brunches, and food fairs to weddings, graduations, mitzvahs, and every celebration worth celebrating, we bring the caviar experience directly to you.

Whether you're planning an intimate dinner or a full-scale event, we'll customize every detail to match your vision and create a moment that feels truly special.

Let us bring the final touch of elegance to your next celebration.
